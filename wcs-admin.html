WCS Administration & Operational Control

Overview

Managed and configured user access, production grouping, and reporting controls within Knapp KiSoft WCS to support operational workflows and reporting visibility.

Production Group Management

  • Created production groups aligned with operational workflows
  • Assigned users based on role and function (Sort, Pack, etc.)
  • Structured group hierarchy to reflect floor operations

This allowed clear separation of responsibilities and improved operational coordination across stations.

Role-Based Permissions

  • SLS (Supervisory) groups with elevated access
  • KPI groups for reporting and performance tracking
  • Station-level permissions (Sort, Pack operations)
  • Controlled access to WCS functions and interfaces

Permissions were structured to ensure users only accessed relevant functionality while maintaining operational security.

Statistical Reporting Configuration

  • Created statistical admin groups for reporting access
  • Configured report visibility by role and function
  • Enabled operational metrics tracking through WCS reporting tools

Station & Label Configuration

  • Configured station-level report parameters
  • Enabled direct station printing for production reports
  • Supported custom label generation for operational workflows

This allowed real-time reporting and labeling directly at the point of operation.

Impact

Improved system usability, operational efficiency, and reporting accuracy by aligning WCS configuration with real-world production workflows.

Why This Matters

This work demonstrates ownership of application-level administration, user access control, and operational system design—bridging IT and production environments.
